# Refund Policy

**Last Updated: February 15, 2026**

At Clinic Master, we strive to provide the highest quality dental care. This policy outlines our procedures for refunds and cancellations.

## 1. Appointment Cancellations
- Cancellations made more than 24 hours in advance are eligible for a full refund of any deposits paid.
- Cancellations made less than 24 hours in advance may be subject to a cancellation fee.

## 2. Dental Procedures
Refunds for dental procedures are handled on a case-by-case basis. If you are unsatisfied with the results of a treatment, please contact us to discuss potential solutions or adjustments.

## 3. Product Returns
Dental hygiene products purchased at our clinic may be returned within 14 days of purchase if they are unopened and in their original packaging.

## 4. Processing Refunds
Refunds will be processed through the original method of payment within 7-10 business days.
