**Last Updated: February 15, 2026**

Clinic Master uses cookies to enhance your browsing experience and provide personalized services.

## 1. What are Cookies?
Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help the website remember your preferences and improve performance.

## 2. Types of Cookies We Use
- **Essential Cookies**: Necessary for the website to function correctly (e.g., session management).
- **Performance Cookies**: Help us understand how visitors interact with our site.
- **Functional Cookies**: Remember your choices (e.g., language preferences).

## 3. Managing Your Preferences
You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Please note that disabling certain cookies may affect the functionality of our website.

## 4. Consent
By using our website, you consent to our use of cookies as described in this policy.
